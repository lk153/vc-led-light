LED light e-commerce website has these pages:

- **Login and Registration**: A modern split-screen layout with an atmospheric lighting image on one side and a clean toggle-based form for login or sign-up.

- **LED Showroom Home Page**: A high-tech landing page featuring a hero banner, product categories, and a showcase of featured lighting solutions.

- **Product Search and Filters**: A functional search results page with a comprehensive sidebar for filtering by price, brand, wattage, and color temperature.

- **Checkout Cart Summary**: A clear overview of the shopping cart with price totals, promo code entry, and a direct path to the shipping process.

- **Shipping Information**: A structured form for entering delivery details, complete with a progress stepper to guide the user through checkout.

- **Billing Information**: A clean billing address form that appears if the user needs a different address than the shipping one.

- **Payment Method Selection**: A secure payment interface offering various options like credit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets.

- **Order Success Confirmation**: A post-purchase "Thank You" screen showing the order number, summary, and options to track the delivery.

- **Product Detail Page**: A deep-dive page for specific lights, including high-res galleries, technical specifications, and customer reviews.

- **User Profile Dashboard**: A clean desktop dashboard featuring a side navigation menu, personal information summary, and quick widgets for tracking total orders and reward points.

- **Order History List**: A detailed chronological view of past purchases, with clear status indicators (like 'Delivered' or 'Processing') and easy access to tracking and re-ordering features.

- **Product Wishlist Page**: A dedicated section titled 'My Saved Lights' showing a grid of favorited products. Each card includes a product image, rating, price, and clear actions to 'Move to Cart' or share the wishlist with others.

- **Customer Help and Support Center**: A comprehensive support hub for desktop users. It features a prominent search bar, categorized help cards (like 'Order Tracking' and 'Installation Guides'), a section for popular FAQs, and clear options for contacting support through live chat, email, or phone.
